# Product Mockup Generator - Quick Guide

## How It Works

This tool places your logo on products with perfect perspective using mathematics (no AI required).

---

## Basic Steps

1. **Fill in product details** (top card)
   - Product title
   - Description
   - Print size (e.g., 10x10cm)
   - Number of colors

2. **Upload images** (bottom right)
   - Product image
   - Logo (PNG with transparent background recommended)

3. **Adjust logo**
   - Drag logo to reposition on canvas
   - Adjust size with slider (07)
   - Rotate if needed (08)

4. **Choose render mode**
   - **2D Overlay**: Fast, for flat products
   - **Perspective**: Manual corner adjustment for 3D products

5. **Download** your mockup

---

## Render Modes

### 2D Overlay (Recommended for most cases)
- Instant rendering
- Perfect for flat products (business cards, stickers, boxes)
- Drag logo to position, adjust size and rotation

### Perspective Mode
- For 3D products with curved or angled surfaces
- Drag the corner handles to match the product's perspective
- Click inside to move the entire logo
- Drag corners to adjust perspective distortion

---

## Tips

✅ Use PNG logos with transparent backgrounds for best results  
✅ For flat products, use 2D Overlay mode  
✅ For 3D products, use Perspective mode and adjust corners  
✅ All changes update in real-time on the canvas  
✅ Works completely offline - no internet needed  

---

## Troubleshooting

**Logo not visible?**  
→ Make sure both product and logo images are uploaded

**Can't reposition logo?**  
→ Make sure you've uploaded both images first

**Download button disabled?**  
→ Upload both product and logo images to enable download
